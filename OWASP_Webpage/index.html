Welcome

Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) has been a steady source of highly skilled talent. OWASP Student Chapter, one of the leading coding societies, consists of problem solvers eager to innovate. Through hackathons, tech-talks, workshops, and coding nights, we strive to uphold the campus coding culture.

Participation

OWASP is a nonprofit foundation dedicated to software security. Our projects, tools, documents, and forums are open to all. Chapters follow the Chapters Policy, and financial contributions should only be made through authorized channels.

We encourage participation in Projects, Local Chapters, Events, Online Groups, and Slack. OWASP offers a platform to learn, network, and build expertise. Consider becoming a member or donating to support our mission.
